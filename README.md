This is basically dozer but system wide and if you move it puts any of the punishments you chose, the default one is focus window close

If you don't know what dozer is, basically what you need to do is just don't move when he appears, when he opens your eye and you move your mouse/press a key, dozer will attack.

## stuff it does
It has 3 forms of punishments, with the version uploaded rn theres shutdown , close window you were previously using, shutdown pc completely or harmless
It has an ui openable with f6, shutdown everything with f5

you will be able to modify the chances and cooldown too, with my preset being recommended for challenges, i think
